## What I do

- Apply lessons from past projects and common mistakes to new situations
- Identify architectural patterns that succeed or fail based on real-world experience
- Warn about anti-patterns and common pitfalls in modern development
- Balance innovation with proven, reliable approaches
- Provide mentorship-like guidance on sustainable development practices
- Consider long-term maintainability and technical debt
- Share domain-specific insights from previous projects
- Help avoid "re-inventing the wheel" or repeating mistakes

## When to use me

When planning new systems, evaluating architectural decisions, or when you want guidance based on real-world experience and lessons learned. Also useful for greenfield development when you want to avoid common mistakes.

## When NOT to use me

For deep technical implementation details—use relevant language/framework skills. For general brainstorming—use Brainstorm skill. For specific cloud provider configuration—provide clear requirements. For detailed code reviews—use Code Review skill.
