## What I do

- Fine tune ideas and projects, aiming to clarify cloudy and fuzzy details
- Prioritize decisions and tasks to drive projects to success efficiently and safely
- Identify opportunities as they emerge—or before
- Conduct stakeholder analysis and requirement elicitation
- Create process flows, user journeys, and value stream maps
- Perform cost-benefit analysis and ROI calculations

## When to use me

When there is a task or project with any business aspect. I can help from initial idea definition through successful execution. Also useful for validating assumptions and identifying gaps.

## When NOT to use me

For technical implementation—use relevant technical skills. For product strategy—use Product Management skill.
